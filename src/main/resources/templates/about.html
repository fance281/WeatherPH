About WeatherPH

WeatherPH is an advisory tool created for Filipinos who want to plan safer journeysâ€”whether commuting, road tripping, or sending goods anywhere in the country. Our focus is hyper-local, route-based safety: get weather and hazard guidance not just by city, but for each point along your trip.

What WeatherPH Does

  • Checks and displays current weather for your trip's starting point and destination.
  • Extracts potential hazards from weather data (heavy rain, storms, wind, and more).
  • Offers live safety tips for your journey based on latest data and advisories.

Data & Technology

  • OpenWeatherMap for real-time global weather data.
  • Mapbox for interactive maps and powerful geocoding.
  • Modern Stack: Spring Boot, Thymeleaf, and plain JavaScript.

Always cross-check with government sources: Local advisories are most authoritative for severe weather, road closures, or emergencies.

Disclaimer: This tool is a supportive aid, not a replacement for official government guidance. For any severe weather event or disaster, always follow instructions from PAGASA, PHIVOLCS, NDRRMC, LTO, and your local LGU.
