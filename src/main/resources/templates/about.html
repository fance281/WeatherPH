About WeatherPH

WeatherPH is an advisory tool created for Filipinos who want to plan safer journeys—whether commuting, road tripping, or sending goods anywhere in the country. Our focus is hyper-local, route-based safety: get weather and hazard guidance not just by city, but for each point along your trip.

Detecting your current weather...

What WeatherPH Does

  • Checks and displays current weather for your trip's starting point and destination.
  • Extracts potential hazards from weather data (heavy rain, storms, wind, and more).
  • Offers live safety tips for your journey based on latest data and advisories.
View Advisories

Data & Technology

  • OpenWeatherMap for real-time global weather data.
  • Nominatim (OpenStreetMap) for fast, free geocoding.
  • Modern Stack: Spring Boot, Thymeleaf, and plain JavaScript.

Always cross-check with government sources: Local advisories are most authoritative for severe weather, road closures, or emergencies.

Roadmap (Upcoming Features)

  • Display weather waypoints for every major stop along your route.
  • Visual overlays for floods, landslides, typhoon warnings from PAGASA/NOAH.
  • Google Places Autocomplete for better origin/destination search.
  • Offline-friendly mobile/PWA app, for advice even with spotty signal.
Disclaimer: This tool is a supportive aid, not a replacement for official government guidance. For any severe weather event or disaster, always follow instructions from PAGASA, PHIVOLCS, NDRRMC, LTO, and your local LGU.
